Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data CloudAI collects, how we use it, and what choices you have. We've written this in plain English — no legal jargon, no hidden surprises.

The Short Version

✅ We don't require you to create an account.

✅ We don't sell your personal data to anyone.

✅ Chat conversations are processed in real-time and not permanently stored on our servers.

✅ We use Google Analytics to understand how people use the site (anonymous data only).

✅ We may show ads through Google AdSense to keep the service free.

What We Collect

Chat Messages: When you send a message to CloudAI, it's sent to an AI service for processing. We don't permanently store your conversations on our servers. Once the response is generated, the chat exists only in your browser session.

Files & Images: If you upload an image or file, it's processed temporarily to generate a response. We don't keep copies of your uploads.

Analytics Data: We use Google Analytics to understand general usage patterns — things like how many people visit, which pages are popular, and what devices are used. This data is anonymous and doesn't identify you personally.

Cookies: Google Analytics and Google AdSense may place cookies on your device. These are standard cookies used by millions of websites. You can manage or block cookies through your browser settings.

How We Use Your Data

Your messages are used only to generate AI responses. Analytics data helps us improve the site. That's it. We don't build profiles, we don't track you across the web, and we don't share your data with third parties beyond what's needed to run the service.

Third-Party Services

CloudAI uses the following third-party services:

AI Processing: Your messages are sent to an AI API for response generation. The AI provider may process your messages according to their own privacy policies.

Google Analytics: For anonymous usage statistics. Google's Privacy Policy

Google AdSense: For displaying ads. Google may use cookies to show relevant ads. How Google uses data

Your Choices

You can clear your browser data at any time to remove any locally stored information. You can disable cookies in your browser settings. You can use the site without providing any personal information.

Children's Privacy

CloudAI is not designed for children under 13. We don't knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we'll remove it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes to this policy, we'll update this page with a new date. We encourage you to check back occasionally.
