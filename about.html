How It Works

Just type your question or message, and CloudAI responds instantly. You can also use voice input — tap the microphone and speak naturally. CloudAI supports text, images, and file attachments too.

Who Built This?

CloudAI is built and maintained by Suraj Ahir under SRJahir Tech. We're a small team passionate about making technology accessible and useful for everyone. We also run stocks.srjahir.in — a free Indian stock market data portal.

Our Promise

CloudAI is free to use. We don't sell your data. We don't require accounts. We just want to build something genuinely useful. If you have feedback, ideas, or run into any issues, we'd love to hear from you.
