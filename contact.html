Frequently Asked Questions

Is CloudAI free?

Yes, completely free. No sign-up, no subscription, no hidden charges.

Does CloudAI save my conversations?

No. Conversations are processed in real-time and exist only in your current browser session. Once you close the tab, they're gone.

Can I use CloudAI on mobile?

Absolutely. CloudAI works on any device with a browser — phone, tablet, or desktop. You can even install it as an app using the "Add to Home Screen" option.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Send us an email with a description of what happened, and we'll look into it as soon as we can.

Can CloudAI understand images?

Yes! You can upload an image using the attach button, and CloudAI will analyze and respond based on what it sees.

Response Time

We're a small team, so it might take us a day or two to get back to you. But we do read every message and appreciate every bit of feedback.
