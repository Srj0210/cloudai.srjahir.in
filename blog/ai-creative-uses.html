10 Creative Things You Can Do With AI

By SRJahir Tech · March 2026 · 6 min read

Most people use AI for basic questions — "What's the weather?" or "Explain this concept." But that barely scratches the surface. Here are ten genuinely creative and useful things you might not have thought of.

1. Write a Short Story Together

Tell the AI a premise — "A detective in 2080s Mumbai discovers that the city's AI traffic system is hiding something" — and let it write the opening. Then take turns adding to the story. It's collaborative fiction, and it's surprisingly fun. You can set the genre, the tone, the characters, and see where the story goes.

2. Practice for Job Interviews

Tell AI: "You're a hiring manager at a tech company. Interview me for a software developer role. Ask me one question at a time and give feedback on my answers." It'll conduct a realistic mock interview, point out where your answers could be stronger, and help you prepare without the pressure of a real interviewer.

3. Learn a New Language

AI is like a patient language tutor who never gets tired. Ask it to have a conversation in Spanish. Ask it to correct your Hindi grammar. Tell it to explain why certain French phrases work the way they do. You can practice reading, writing, and even get pronunciation tips — all for free.

💡 Try: "Let's have a simple conversation in Japanese. Start with basic phrases and correct my mistakes gently."

4. Plan a Detailed Trip

Instead of spending hours on travel blogs, tell AI where you're going, how many days you have, your budget, and what kind of activities you enjoy. It'll create a day-by-day itinerary with restaurant suggestions, travel tips, and local insights. You can then refine it — "Add more food spots" or "Make day 3 more relaxed."

5. Debug Your Thinking

Have a big decision to make? Explain your situation to AI and ask it to play devil's advocate. "I'm thinking of quitting my job to start a business. What am I not considering? What could go wrong?" AI can help you see blind spots in your reasoning without any judgment or bias.

6. Create a Workout or Meal Plan

"I'm vegetarian, want to lose 5kg in 3 months, and can work out 4 days a week at home with no equipment." AI can create a personalized workout schedule and meal plan tailored to your exact situation. It's not a replacement for a professional trainer, but it's a fantastic starting point.

7. Summarize Books and Podcasts

Don't have time to read a 400-page book? Ask AI for a detailed summary of its key ideas. Want to know if a podcast episode is worth your time? Describe the topic and AI can give you the main takeaways. It's like having a well-read friend who gives you the highlights.

8. Write Better Emails and Messages

Struggling with how to phrase something? Give AI the context and it'll draft it for you. "Write a polite email declining a meeting invitation because I have a conflict" or "Help me respond to this message from my landlord about a maintenance issue — I want to be firm but respectful." It handles tone perfectly.

9. Brainstorm Business or Project Ideas

"I know web development and live in a small city in Gujarat. What are some side project or small business ideas that could work for me?" AI can generate dozens of ideas tailored to your skills, location, and interests. Then you can drill down into any idea — "Tell me more about idea #3. How would I start?"

10. Explain Things to Kids

If you're a parent or teacher, AI is incredible for generating age-appropriate explanations. "Explain gravity to a 6-year-old" or "How do I explain why the sky is blue to a curious 8-year-old?" You get simple, engaging explanations that actually make sense to young minds — complete with analogies and examples.

The Only Limit Is Your Imagination

AI is a tool that adapts to whatever you need. The more creative your prompts, the more creative the results. Don't just ask questions — give it scenarios, challenges, and creative constraints. Try CloudAI and see what you can come up with.
